My PRK Experience: Pre-Operation

Thursday, April 9th 2015



Tomorrow, I'm having my PRK surgery done on my right eye after work. I'm pretty nervous/excited/anxious/scared + a million other emotions all rolled into one. I hope there's no pain and that I heal quickly so I can get my left eye done asap. A few of my friends and co-workers were telling me about their experience and they made it seem like a piece of cake, but they all had LASIK which is slightly different that PRK. My healing time is much slower since the entire flap has to grow back, unlike LASIK where they lift, operate, and put the flap back down. I started putting antibiotic drops in my eye yesterday as per the doctor, and tomorrow they'll recheck my eyes and give me a Xanax before the surgery. I hope there are no complications, wish me luck!
